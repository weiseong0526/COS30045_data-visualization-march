Welcome to Appliance Energy Consumption Insights

This website provides information on energy consumption of appliances in the Australian market. Appliances like televisions, refrigerators, and air conditioners contribute significantly to household energy use.

In Australia, the average household spends about $1,500 per year on electricity, with appliances accounting for a large portion. Understanding energy ratings and usage can help reduce costs and environmental impact.

Explore the sections to learn more about specific appliances and tips for energy efficiency.

Television Energy Consumption

Televisions are a common appliance in Australian homes. Modern LED TVs are more energy-efficient than older models.

The average Australian household owns multiple TVs, consuming around 10-15% of household electricity. Choosing energy-rated models can save up to 50% on energy costs.

Tips: Turn off TVs when not in use, use power-saving modes, and consider screen size for energy efficiency.
